Derrick Husmann, Vice President of Operations has been with PF Management Services since March of 2016. He brings 24 years of experience in the funeral profession as a licensed funeral director/embalmer and served 8 years as a Batesville Sales Representative in Illinois and Missouri. Derrick earned a Bachelor’s of Science Degree in Mortuary Science and Funeral Service from Southern Illinois University.
